About Us

Giraffe R Us was founded in April of 2013. The company is owned by two young, adventerous, handsome, smart, charming, muscular, brave, couragous, kind, nice, etc. men. Brendan Liffers and Nic Jones have both gotten their doctorates in animal science, and have dedicated their lives to research of the Giraffe. Taking countless trips to Africa, Austrialia, New Zealand, Antarctica, North Korea, and even the Salisbury Zoo. We've given our lives to Giraffes, and we hope to discover a lot more about these creatures in the near future. We thank you for coming to our site and taking your time to look at the research we've gathered about the endangered Giraffe!
